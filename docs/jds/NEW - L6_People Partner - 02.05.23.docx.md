| **Main Purpose of the role** |  |
| The People Partner works within the People Partnering team to support the delivery of the overarching people plan of the Business Unit and Centrica people strategy. The People Partner works at pace to diagnose, design and deliver the best People solutions required for delivering the people plan, linking in with People Consultants, Centres of Excellence and other HR shared services to leverage key expertise / ensure consistent delivery.  Working as part of a flexible people partnering team ensuring business intimacy & commerciality to provide business support where needed. Providing professional end to end HR expertise, coaching & support to business leaders.   |  |
| **Key Accountabilities of the role** |  |
| Be a trusted advisor and a strong people generalist enabling the business to deliver for our customers Understand the business strategy and therefore people plan requirements and translate into practical delivery Provide practical generalist people expertise, guidance and coaching to leaders Effectively manage a range of people deliverables including lifecycle activities such as ARR, Talent management and performance, organisational change & transformation, TUPE, reward programmes, employee engagement, building capability and other people related topics Building & leveraging relationships with wider people partnering teams both within the business and across Centrica, people consultants, business leaders, people shared services and Centres of Excellence to access specialist advice and input as required and to ensure wider knowledge share, reducing the potential for siloed working Seek out external best practice & continually develop to grow people expertise |  |
